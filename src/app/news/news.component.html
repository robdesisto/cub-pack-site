Cub Scouts Information & Registration Night

Wednesday, September 6, 6:30 - 7:30PM
Village Hall, Village Elementary (enter on H Ave between 6th & 7th)

Cub Scouts has now expanded to allow Kindergarteners who will be entering First Grade next year to participate, through our Lion program! Now all boys in Kindergarten through Fifth Grade can enjoy Cub Scouts. Come visit us at our annual “Round-Up” to learn what Scouting can offer your family, meet our leaders, and visit with the Scouts!

You can also visit our new family orientation and mixer at Spreckels Park on Saturday, September 9, from 11-12.
